Header Zone Text

The text printed to the right of the barcodes at the top of each ballot. The headline is large and bold (e.g. "OFFICIAL BALLOT"). The body text can span multiple paragraphs — use a literal backslash-n ( ) between paragraphs. Supported tokens replaced automatically at print time: {electionName}, {jurisdictionName}, {indicatorName} (oval / box / arrow / number box).
