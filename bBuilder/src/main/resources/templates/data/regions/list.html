SinglePrecinct regions are individual voting precincts — voters are registered to exactly one and receive a ballot based on it. PrecinctGroup regions (Water Districts, Cities, School Districts, etc.) group multiple SinglePrecincts together; a contest assigned to a PrecinctGroup automatically appears on every member SinglePrecinct's ballot.
