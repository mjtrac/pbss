How it works
  1. The bounding-box report (XML or YAML) gives the theoretical position of every vote indicator on the ballot, expressed as nested offsets.
  2. For each scanned image, the QR/barcode is decoded to identify the ballot page.
  3. The heavy outer border of the content area is located in the image.
  4. A perspective transform maps the scanned box to the theoretical rectangle, correcting for skew, rotation, and scale.
  5. Dark pixel fractions inside each indicator box are measured to detect marks.
  6. Results are tallied across all images and shown as a vote count report.
